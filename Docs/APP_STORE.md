## Promotional text (≤ 170 chars)
A cozy pixel-art garden that grows as you note small good things — and never wilts when life gets in the way. Forgiving by design.

## Description
Gratitude Garden is a quiet place to notice one small thing a day.

Each entry helps a hand-drawn pixel garden grow. On good days, write something you're grateful for. On harder days, note something you got through, or something you're looking forward to — there's no wrong entry, and no day you "have" to show up.

The garden is forgiving on purpose. Miss a day or two and nothing happens. Step away longer and it simply rests — it never dies, and your progress is never lost. Whenever you come back, it begins to bloom again, and welcomes you.

There are no streaks to break, no guilt, and no pressure. Just a calm, warm space that's happy to see you whenever you visit.

• A living pixel-art garden that reflects your practice
• Gratitude, "got through," or "looking forward to" — your choice each day
• A home-screen widget so your garden is always a glance away
• Optional, gentle reminders worded as invitations, never nagging
• A calm journal of everything you've noted, with search
• Fully private: everything stays on your device

No account. No tracking. No cloud. Your words are yours.
