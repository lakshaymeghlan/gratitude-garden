# Drop your world art here

Paste your 7 landscape images into THIS folder, named exactly:

    world_0.png   ← sparsest (bare meadow, hopeful)
    world_1.png
    world_2.png
    world_3.png
    world_4.png
    world_5.png
    world_6.png   ← lushest (magical flower valley)

Notes:
- PNG, landscape orientation (roughly 3:2 or 4:3). High-res is fine (e.g. 1500×1000+).
- world_0 = least grown, world_6 = most grown. The image itself shows the progression.
- Once pasted, tell Claude "art is in" and it will install them into WorldArt.xcassets
  (both the app and the widget will then use them automatically).
